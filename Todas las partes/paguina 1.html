Analysis

According to the Medellín Traffic Department, the Manrique sector with the 45 is one of the areas of the city with the highest number of accidents, due to the recklessness of motorcyclists and pedestrians who use the exclusive Metroplús lane, breaking traffic regulations. The road education team took this sector to teach drivers and pedestrians the importance of using platforms, zebras, traffic lights, in addition to safety elements when driving a motorcycle

In 2015, 32 thousand injured people appeared in the city, and around 25 thousand were motorcyclists, and of 289 people who died, 60% were pedestrians and 40% motorcyclists. The campaign included a pedagogical intervention accompanied by the BTL de sombra The aim is to show citizens the mortality and accident figures on the roads.
